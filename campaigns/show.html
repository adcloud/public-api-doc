GET https://api.adcloud.com/v2/campaigns/:id
Show a campaign

To get the details of a Campaign simply perform a GET action to the url specified and provide the id of the Campaign you wish to view.

Examples

Request: 
GET https://api.adcloud.com/v2/campaigns/1/?access_token={ACCESS_TOKEN}
